I've graduated from IIT Hyderabad with a Bachelor of Technology in 2024, setting up to join Columbia University this Fall '24!

During my undergrad, I've worked with Dr. Ayon Borthkaur to create an algorithm which trains neural networks without the need for backpropagation. I extensively studied local learning, few shot learning, and few epoch learning, incorporating the algorithm with all these features. I've had the pleasure to work with Prof. Sumohana Channappayya on digital video compression. I came up with a novel transform inspired by field dynamics which shows improved performance over the traditional DCT, especially in texture rich regions. Since then I've explored tons of graph compression algorithms with Dr. Shashank Vatedka and more recently, worked on very high dimensional data compression, aiming to reduce transmission to less than 1 bit per dimension.

During the first few years of my virtual undergrad (caused by the pandemic), I worked at Anitech Solutions mentored by CEO Andrew Nolan where I worked with the Engineering team and Business Analysts to deliver and elaborate on functional and non functional requirements of KnowCost for JIRA. For the summer of 2023, I worked at Oracle India as an software intern, where I helped build on Redwood dependency analyzer for Redwood Infrastructure Components and Services (RADAR).

No doubt, the best way to learn is to teach. I've represented IIT Hyderabad at Nihilanth, the Inter-IIT-IIM Quiz Championship, held at IIM Calcutta in January 2019, and at The Inter IIT Cultural Meet held at IIT Bombay in Decmber 2019, and at The Inter IIT Tech Meet held by IIT Guwahati in March 2021.
